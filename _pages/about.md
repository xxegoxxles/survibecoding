This is where I synthesize what I'm learning about building AI agents and using AI agents to build things.

I started writing these notes for teammates and friends who were trying to understand AI and did not know where to start. I do not consider myself an AI expert or researcher. I'm an engineer who likes to build things, loves technology, loves sharing knowledge, and has been experimenting with AI-assisted development and agentic workflows since 2024.

Some posts will be primers and basic explanations meant to help people get oriented. Others will be deeper notes on current ideas I run into while learning, breaking, building, and survibecoding in the age of AI.
